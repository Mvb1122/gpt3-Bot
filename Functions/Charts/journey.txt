journey
    title My working day
    section Go to work
      Make tea: 5: Me
      Go upstairs: 3: Me
      Do work: 1: Me, Cat
    section Go home
      Go downstairs: 5: Me
      Sit down: 5: Me

The names after the colon on each event represent the people who do that event. They must match previous events' users where applicable. Not every event has to have every person.
The numbers after the first colon are the person's happiness from 1 to 5 at that point.

Here's another example: 

journey
    title Daily Routine
    section Go to School
      Wake up: 5: Me, Cat
      Eat Breakfast: 4: Me, Cat
      Walk to class: 4: Me
    section Class
      Japanese: 5: Me
      Calculus: 2: Me
      Computer Science: 4: Me
